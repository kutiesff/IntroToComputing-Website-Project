Archery is the ancient art and sport of using a bow to shoot arrows at a target. It's a skill that blends precision, focus, and technique, requiring both physical prowess and mental discipline.

Volleyball

Volleyball is an energetic team sport played on a rectangular court divided by a high net. Players aim to score points by grounding the ball on the opponent's side of the court.

Track and Field

Track and field is a diverse and exhilarating sport that encompasses various athletic events, typically held on a track or field area. It includes a wide range of disciplines such as running, jumping, throwing, and hurdling. From sprints and long-distance races to high jumps, pole vaults, discus throws, and more, track and field showcases individual and team-based athleticism. Athletes specialize in specific events, showcasing their speed, strength, agility, and technique. The sport's appeal lies in its combination of raw physical prowess, technical skill, and the pursuit of personal and competitive excellence across a multitude of disciplines.
